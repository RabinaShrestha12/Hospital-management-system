About EKA Hospital

EKA Hospital is a multi-specialty healthcare institution committed to providing high-quality, compassionate, and affordable medical services. With cutting-edge facilities and a team of highly trained professionals, we strive to make healthcare accessible to everyone, every day.

Why Choose MedLife Hospital?

Choosing EKA Hospital means choosing trust, technology, and timely treatment. Our hospital is known for its commitment to excellence, patient-first policy, and ethical standards. We continuously invest in innovation, training, and medical research to stay ahead in delivering quality care.

What Makes Us the Best

  • Experienced and specialized doctors in every department
  • State-of-the-art medical technology and labs
  • 24/7 emergency and ambulance services
  • Digital patient records and online appointment system
  • Clean, safe, and patient-friendly environment

Facilities We Offer

  • Modern ICU and surgical units
  • Radiology and diagnostic imaging (CT, MRI, X-Ray)
  • Pharmacy and lab services within the hospital
  • Private and general wards with 24-hour nursing
  • Physiotherapy, maternity, and child care units

Our Care for Patients

At EKA, every patient is treated with dignity, respect, and empathy. We believe healing goes beyond just physical recovery — we support mental well-being, listen to our patients’ concerns, and involve families in the treatment process. Your comfort and recovery are our top priorities.
